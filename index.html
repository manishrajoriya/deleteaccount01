How to Delete Your Account

This page explains how you can delete your account from App.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the app on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings menu (usually found in the top-right corner).
  3. Select Account or Profile.
  4. Tap on Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your decision by following the on-screen instructions.

What Happens After Deletion?

Once you delete your account:

  • All your personal data and content will be permanently removed.
  • You will no longer have access to your account or any associated services.
